Where should I stay?

DC isn't that big, so really anywhere in the District or Northern Virginia will work, as long as you're willing to take a cab or public transit. We recommend folks book their hotels sooner rather than later since there is a change 3/25 overlaps with the beginning of cherry blossom season.

How do I get to Birch + Barley?

You can access the restaurant via the 52 and 54 buses (north/south) and G2 bus (east/west), several stops on the green and yellow Metro lines, bike share, and Uber/Lyft/DC cab. There is no convenient parking near the restaurant.
