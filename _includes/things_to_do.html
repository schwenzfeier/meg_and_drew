Things to do in DC

Sips + Snacks

  • Tryst: coffee + good place to hang out
  • Bluejacket Brewing: good beer + good food
  • Right Proper: beer + quality cheese plate
  • Ethopic: Ethiopian food
  • The Little Grand: pizza -- get "the square"!
  • Thip Khao: Lao food, beware of spice
  • Supra: Georgian (the country) food
  • Mah-Ze-Dahr Bakery: delicious pastries
  • Great Wall Szechuan: home of Meg's favorite dish in the world -- ma po tofu (Drew gets the ma la Kung Pao)
  • The Gibson: speakeasy, go through to the back patio if it's nice weather
  • Daikaya: ramen

Sees + Dos

  • National Museum of African American History and Culture: the newest Smithsonian!
  • National Air and Space Museum: newly renovated!
  • Capital Bikeshare: rent a bikeshare and ride around the mall
  • Cherry blossoms: they might be blooming!
  • Movement Climbing Gym: bouldering
  • Southwest Duck Pond: visit our sometimes loud neighbors
  • Kramers: great bookstore with a cafe/bar attached
